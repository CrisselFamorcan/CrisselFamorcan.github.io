Ledger Lane — Bookkeeping App for Freelancers

Help center articles and onboarding copy for a fintech startup

3–5 days turnaround
Client BriefA fintech startup needs clear, friendly help center articles for their bookkeeping app targeting non-finance-savvy freelancers. Tone: plain English, friendly but professional. No jargon. Make complicated things feel simple.

Help Article 1 — "How do I connect my bank account?"

Connecting your bank account to Ledger Lane takes less than 2 minutes and lets the app automatically import and categorize your transactions — so you spend less time on admin and more time on your actual work.

  1. Go to Settings → Connected Accounts
  2. Tap Add Bank Account
  3. Search for your bank by name
  4. Enter your online banking credentials (we use 256-bit encryption — your data is always secure)
  5. Select which account(s) to sync and tap Confirm

Your transactions will appear within a few minutes and sync automatically every 24 hours.

Help Article 2 — "What's the difference between an expense and a deduction?"

Here's the simple version: an expense is any money you spend for your business. A deduction is an expense that qualifies to reduce your taxable income. Not every expense is automatically a deduction — it has to be "ordinary and necessary" for your type of work. Ledger Lane flags potential deductions automatically, but we recommend confirming with a tax professional before filing.
